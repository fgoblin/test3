Tokenomics and Special Features

Re-distribution to holders (Reflection)

7% of each transaction will be redistributed to all existing MarsDoge holders as a passive income, based on the number of tokens held by them.

Auto allocation to Liquidity Pool

1% of each transaction will be allocated into the Liquidity pool on the PancakeSwap. Hence it will be helping to maintain the price flow on the transactions.

Massive Marketing & Development Goals

1% of each transaction will be assigned to the development wallet. It will be used for the future development of the project as specified in the Whitepaper.

Auto Burn

1% of each transaction will be burned by transferring tokens to the dead wallet to decrease the circulating supply along with time and increase the price of MarsDoge.

Limited Supply

The maximum token supply will only be 225,000,000 MarsDoge. Hence Mars Doge token has a high probability of skyrocketing the price within a short amount of time.

Security Features

Liquidity will be locked forever on PinkSale and the ownership will be transferred to a Timelock contract. Further details are available in the whitepaper.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

✔ MarsDoge is a deflationary, reflection token deployed on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC). It allows users to earn a passive income simply by holding MarsDoge.

✔ MarsDoge token already verified on Binance Smart Chain, and the contract address is “0xd76f84a1D7B9628280f9e9C6910866CECDc7f3A5”.

✔ The ownership of the MarsDoge token contract has transferred to a time-locked contract with 48-hour latency. So, the holders will be aware of the privileged operations in advance, and they can take action accordingly. (https://bscscan.com/address/0x840c78255069f95920cCcCe0578ACE7F64dcfb56)

✔ The proposer role of the Timelock contract has assigned to a Multi-Signature wallet with a 3/5 threshold, to prevent a single point of failure due to mishandling of the private key. (https://bscscan.com/address/0x87AB786FAc232806b3b58d7d003850E525c8a7E0)

✔ To protect the LP tokens from being stolen, accessing LP tokens is restricted by assigning the contract itself as the receiver of LP tokens on adding Liquidity.

✔ Initial liquidity locked forever on the PinkSale.

✔ In order to minimize/break major whale actions, the maximum transaction amount has been limited to One million MarsDoge tokens.

✔ You just need to add the contract address of the MarsDoge token “0xd76f84a1D7B9628280f9e9C6910866CECDc7f3A5” to your wallet. That’s it!. Rewards will be automatically distributed to the holders.

✔ The total number of tokens is limited to 225 million, which has already been generated. This number will be reduced further due to the auto burn function and the owner cannot generate further tokens in the MarsDoge contract.

✔ Read our Whitepaper and refer to the MarsDoge documents for further clarification.

✔ You can send an email to contact@marsdogetoken.com or drop a message on any of our social media accounts, and we are always ready to help you!
